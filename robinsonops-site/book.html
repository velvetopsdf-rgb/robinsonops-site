Questions Before You Book

Is this actually free?

Yes. The 15-minute audit is free, always. I use it to understand your operations and show you what I'd fix. If we're a fit, I'll quote you on the spot. If not, you still leave with clarity on your biggest operational leak.

What if I'm not sure I need this?

That's exactly who this call is for. If you're losing time to manual scheduling, chasing invoices, or your team runs on text messages and memory — there's something to fix. The call tells you what it is and whether it's worth fixing.

Do I need to prepare anything?

No prep needed. Just show up ready to describe how your day-to-day operations work — how jobs get booked, how you invoice, how your team communicates. I'll ask the right questions.

What happens after the call?

If you want to move forward, we start with the Ops Cleanup Sprint — a 7-day engagement where I audit your systems and deliver working solutions. $750 flat rate, no surprises. If you're not ready, no follow-up pressure.
