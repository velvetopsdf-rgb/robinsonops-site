7-Day Breakdown

What Happens Each Day

Most of this is handled remotely. No disruption to your day — we work around your schedule.

Day 1
Kickoff & Context Intake

60-minute call to walk through your current operations. We map your tools, your team, your workflow, and where you feel the most pain. You don't need to prepare — just talk through a normal week.

Remote Call
Day 2
Process Audit & Leak Identification

Robinson Ops maps every manual touchpoint in your operation and identifies where time, leads, and revenue are escaping. You get a preliminary list of your top 3 leaks by end of day.

Remote Work
Day 3
Automation Plan Sign-Off

30-minute call to review the leak report and agree on which automation gets built first. You pick the priority — we build to your decision, not ours.

Remote Call
Days 4–5
Build & Integration

The automation gets built inside your existing tools — no expensive new software required. Typically this is an n8n workflow connected to your email, your sheets, and your calendar. We test it against real scenarios from your business.

Remote Build
Day 6
Live Testing & Walkthrough

We run the automation live with real data from your business. You watch it work in real time. Any adjustments get made on the spot. We also walk you through the written playbook so you own it, not just rent it.

On-Site or Video
Day 7
Handoff & Sprint Report

Full written sprint report delivered: ops map, audit findings, what was built, how to run it, and what to automate next if you choose to continue. The system is live and running independently before we wrap.
